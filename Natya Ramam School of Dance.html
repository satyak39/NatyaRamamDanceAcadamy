Smt. Prasantha Yelagani

Smt. Prasantha Yelagani, Artistic Director of Natya Ramam Dance Academy, began her journey in Bharatanatyam at the age of four under the guidance of Guru Smt. Susheela Sadashivan. Her passion for dance led her to Kuchipudi, where she built a strong foundation under Guru Smt. Gowri Manjeera, an esteemed disciple of Padma Bhushan Guru Dr. Vempati Chinna Satyam, the doyen of the Vempati style of Kuchipudi.

She holds a Diploma in Kuchipudi under the guidance of Guru Dr. Amukta Maalyada, (a distinguished disciple of Guru Smt. Bala Kondala Rao), Guru Dr. Vasanth Kiran and Guru Smt. Ashwini Nambiar enhancing her artistry and the nuances of Kuchipudi.

Affiliated with SAMPADA (SiliconAndhra,USA) , she continues to mentor and nurture students in the classical dance form of Kuchipudi. During her school and college years, she was honored with several awards at district and state-level competitions organized by YCS/YCM and various government awareness programs. She was also part of dance troupes that performed to raise awareness about contemporary social issues. She presented programs on International visual media platforms including ManaTV and TV5.

Smt. Prasantha Yelagani is deeply committed to preserving the rich tradition of the Guru-Shishya Parampara, mentoring the next generation of dancers to uphold and propagate the classical Indian art form with discipline, dedication, and artistic excellence.

Natya Ramam’s mission is to teach Kuchipudi,Bharatanatyam and Bollywood through a holistic, traditional, and appealing "Guru-Shishya Parampara"(Teacher-Disciple Tradition) for the upliftment of body, mind and soul. We seek to kindle in our dancers the joy of classical dance with Music and Yoga, and instill in them the values of dedication and commitment to the practice and performance of this exquisite art form.

Locations: Hindu Samaj Temple (Mahwah), Sri Guruvayurappan Temple (Marlboro), South Brunswick and Edison, NJ

Course of Study

At Natya Ramam Dance Academy, students undergo a rigorous step-by-step approach to learning Kuchipudi & Bharatanatyam. The course of study is divided into Beginner, Intermediate and Advanced levels.

Beginner Level I

  • Dance Shlokas
  • Basic Adugulu (steps) focusing on Sthaanaka (posture), Drishti (eye movement), and Angashuddhi (neatness)
  • Asamyutha Hastas (hand gestures)
  • Jathis - 1st Set

Beginner Level II

  • Jathis - 2nd Set
  • Adugulu - 2nd Set
  • Chinna Kavuthvam
  • Samyutha Hastas
  • Theory: Shiro Bedha, Drishti Bedha, Greeva Bedha, Bhru Bedha

Intermediate Level I

  • Brahmanjali, Jathiswaram, Ramayana Shabdam
  • Focus on Abhinaya (facial expressions)
  • Theory: Paada Bedha

Intermediate Level II

  • Vinayaka Kavuthvam, Thillana, BhamaKalapam, Shivastakam
  • Nattuvangam training

Advanced Level I

  • Tarangam, Kamakshi Stuti, Mandooka Shabdam
  • Dasavataara Shabdam, Krishna Shabdam, Tuluva Rajendra Shabdam

Advanced Level II

  • Rangapravesha (Arangetram) training
  • Nattuvangam training
  • For dancers with previous training who seek to further their dance careers
